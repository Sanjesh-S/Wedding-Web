The details

Everything you need for Aravindhan & Nimisha’s celebrations in Coimbatore — timings, venue, and how to plan your visit.

Schedule

  • Reception Saturday, 11 April 2026 · 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM Rengaz Mahal, Karumathampatti, Coimbatore
  • Wedding Sunday, 12 April 2026 · 6:00 AM onwards Rengaz Mahal, Karumathampatti, Coimbatore

Timings may be confirmed closer to the date; any updates will be shared with guests.

Dress code

Traditional Indian festive attire is welcome — think silk sarees, veshti & shirt, or elegant Indo-western. Comfortable footwear is a good idea for temple and hall spaces.

Travel

Coimbatore is well connected by air (Coimbatore International Airport — CJB) and rail. Karumathampatti is reachable by road from the city centre; ride-hail and local taxis are widely available. If you need help with routes, reach out to the hosts.
